LAL KITAB



Lal Kitab is a unique field of astrology whose origin has different versions. One of them is believed to be translated by Pandit Roop Chand Joshi. He was an accountant by profession working for british administration .According to one story Book is said to be dictated to him in his dreams by divine powers and as per second story was translated from scripts found while digging at a construction site. Irrespective of its origin, it provides simple ,easy and affordable remedies.

Lal Kitab writings started in business ledger books bound in Red, hence the name Lal Kitab was given to it. The original versions of it are believed to have been written between 1939-1952.

It defines planetary positions and their remedies in form of poetic verses in Urdu script. According to this book our palm lines and planetary positions are same and hence interlinked. Remedies depend on the planet positions offering remedies to Previous Birth debts and current Year chart hence removing the blockages in progress. Right analysis of the planets and their remedies gives instant relief for the existing problems. Truly beneficial and bringing quick results.
