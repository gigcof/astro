About AstroMaestra

Acharya Rachna, I am the founder of Astro Maestra and practices Astrology and Vaastu, Tarot, Lal kitab and Ramal Astrolgy . Everything I am doing today, started long back. I grew up gazing and wondering at the stars, constellations and galaxies in the night sky. Since my childhood, I was a voracious reader and my book shelf always had books related to Palmistry, Numerology, Astrology and related science. It took me quiet long to finally realize my quest was more than a hobby and my compassionate behaviour proved to be a cherry on the cake.

I did my Astrology Course from ICAS followed by Vaastu, Tarot, Lal Kitab from various renowned institutes and teachers. The list is endless. Not to forget, I also ended up completing my postgraduation in Mechanical Engineering in 2007 and working in private sector for 15 years. I have been practicing Astrology and allied subjects for many years but still kindled the desire for something bigger that could help the society. I have always tried to get the most out of the life’s journey along with chasing my dreams.
